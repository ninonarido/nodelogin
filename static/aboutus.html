ABOUT THE SYSTEM

Our web application system is a comprehensive and feature-rich platform designed to provide users with a seamless and efficient online experience. Built on modern technologies and following best practices, our web application system offers a wide range of functionalities to meet the needs of our users.

Atlas is a comprehensive research repository system designed specifically for the students and faculty of UMAK (University of Makat). Serving as a centralized platform, Atlas aims to facilitate the storage, organization, and accessibility of research materials produced by the university's academic community.

The primary objective of Atlas is to provide a robust and user-friendly environment that promotes the dissemination and discovery of knowledge generated through research endeavors. It serves as a digital hub where students and faculty can showcase their scholarly work, collaborate with peers, and contribute to the advancement of various academic disciplines.
